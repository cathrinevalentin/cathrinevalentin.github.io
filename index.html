Profile

My research interests center on digital spaces. With a background in social data science, I examine the political implications of an increasingly online public debate, including its impact on shaping political preferences and behavior.

I am currently conducting an online survey experiment on the political influence of online in-groups, recruiting respondents via social media ads. Before completing the dissertation, I will conduct a multiple-worlds experiment to investigate how small individual-level effects translate into group-level dynamics.
